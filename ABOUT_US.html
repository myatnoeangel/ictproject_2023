Our Campus and Location

Our EDU4U Campus is located in Mahar Aung Myay Township, 2-3 minutes drive from the University of Mandalay. The area of Bawdigone is the hub of students in Mandalay which is home to many tuitions and training centres. There are multiple restaurants and stores within a 5-minute walk so it's convenient for our students to have meals. 

Our Classrooms

Our training campus consists of 4 classrooms that are fully equipped with Smart TVs, Air-conditioners, Whiteboards, etc…
