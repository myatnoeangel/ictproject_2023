Start your Smart Learning at EDU4U

We’re an IGCSE and IAL preparation and training center located in Mandalay. We bring the best out of the students who are willing to achieve their dreams and lead them to a better path of education by filling their needs and producing the best version of them. We believe that every student has the ability to accomplish their goals and we are here to help them achieve it.

We have classes from Secondary 1 to IGCSE and IAL on both online and campus, providing a wide range of subjects to choose from according to their interests. *Please check each class page for available subjects in each relevant level.
